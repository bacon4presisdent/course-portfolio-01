Top 5 Futurama Characters

  1. Fry

    2. Philip J. Fry is the main character of Futurama - a relatable everyman.

  2. Leela

    3. A capable pilot with fierce loyalty, Leela keeps the crew reigned in.

  3. Bender

    4. Whenever the team's spirits are low, Bender is sure to step in and give a swift kick. Honestly, it's basically charity.

  4. Nibbler

    5. Nibbler is an adorable fuzzball with an appetite for trouble, and the fangs to match.

  5. Roberto

    6. Whether he was built to be crazy, or just lucky enough to end up that way, Roberto is a robot to watch out for. Incidentally, you might want to hide your watch.
